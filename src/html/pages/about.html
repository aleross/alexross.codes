My background

I'm a programmer, entrepreneur, speaker, designer, and trail runner among other things. As an engineer I've worked across the stack in backend, web, mobile, sysadmin and more. I live and work in Los Angeles and studied at UCLA.

I'm currently an Engineering Manager working on feature development and engineering best practices alongside a very talented team at Tinder. I'm also an organizer of monthly meetups at the LA CTO Forum, an invite-only organization for technology leaders in Los Angeles.

In 2015 I was featured in Inc.'s 30 under 30 for co-founding Enplug, a display software for teams and businesses. Enplug has hundreds of customers internationally and raised over $5M from prominent investors.

You can find my complete work history and recommendations on LinkedIn, or you can download my resume. I spend some of my free time working on open source software, which you can find on Github.
