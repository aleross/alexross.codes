About

I'm Alex Ross. A programmer, entrepreneur, speaker, designer, trail runner, and aspiring vegan. As an entrepreneur, I've built systems ranging from software to sales comp plans, customer support, supply chains, company culture, and more.

In 2015 I was featured in Inc.'s 30 under 30 for co-founding Enplug, a display software for teams and businesses. Enplug has hundreds of customers internationally and has raised over $5M from prominent investors.

You can find my complete resume and recommendations on LinkedIn. I spend some of my free time working on open source software, which you can find on Github.

Background

I'm a self-taught programmer, and lifelong learner of all things.

I taught myself to program my sophomore year of college. I started by reading the 5th edition (2006) of O'Reilly's Javascript: The Definitive Guide cover to cover over a few weeks. It was a sadistic way to start from scratch, but I didn't know any better and was persistent enough to make it through. Afterwards I built my first website (HTML & CSS) for fun, then volunteered to build a CMS (PHP & MySQL) for an on-campus organization, and soon after got my first programming gig as a web developer (Linux, Apache, PHP & MySQL).

When I helped co-found Enplug in 2012, I was responsible for building our technology as the CTO. I spent a year and a half creating our system architecture and recruiting incredible engineers onto our team. During this time I worked across all levels of the stack, from server allocation and admin to Java/Android applications on the Enplug client devices, and everything in between.

In 2014 Enplug was growing and I became our COO to make sure things didn't break along the way. During this time I became hands-on with each part of our business for several months at a time. Door-to-door small business sales with our sales team. Customer support calls, supply chain management, shipping product with our operations team. Onboarding, communication and events with HR. Advertising, email marketing and blogging with our marketing team.

I helped create systems, promoted leaders, and wrote automation/LoB software. I had a blast diving deep into so many areas of expertise with such great teammates.
