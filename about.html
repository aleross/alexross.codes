About

I'm Alex Ross. A programmer, entrepreneur, speaker, designer, trail runner, and aspiring vegan. As a programmer I work across the stack with a specialty in front-end.

In 2015 I was featured in Inc.'s 30 under 30 for co-founding Enplug, a display software for teams and businesses. Enplug has hundreds of customers internationally and has raised over $5M from prominent investors.

You can find my complete resume and recommendations on LinkedIn. I spend some of my free time working on open source software, which you can find on Github.
